üîß Troubleshooting

Login & Account Issues
  • Can't log in: Check your email and password. Use "Forgot Password" if needed.
  • Not receiving emails: Check your spam folder. Add us to your contacts.
  • Account verification: Click the link in your signup email to verify your account.
Data & Syncing
  • Workouts not saving: Ensure you're logged in and have a stable internet connection.
  • Data not syncing: Refresh the page or log out and back in.
  • Lost workout data: Check your workout history tab or contact support.
Performance Issues
  • Slow loading: Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Page not loading: Try refreshing or using a different browser.
  • Mobile issues: Ensure you're using a supported mobile browser.
Browser Compatibility
  • Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
  • JavaScript required: Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
  • Cookies required: Allow cookies for authentication to work properly
